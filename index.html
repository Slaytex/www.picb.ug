CHRIS JACOBS DESIGN
VIRTUAL INTERFACE DESIGN / DEVELOPMENT / IDENTITY
Chris Jacobs is a User Experience Designer (digital and physical) and Creative Consultant living in Los Angeles. His work has spanned many disciplines from Industrial Design, Architecture and User-Interface Design.
More info on Chris
Chris has founded two design companies, and sold his first to a publicly traded company. He's worked in Los Angeles, Bombay, Hong Kong, Beijing, and Singapore. He was responsible for the interaction and user-interface design for Interpublic Group Emerging Media Lab's latest "Glass touch screen" technology for the retail industry, which is IPG's flagship touch-point product on display in the Los Angeles and New York offices. He co-founded the Interactive company United Future (Culver City) in 2006, staying until 2011. He currently works as Interactive Creative Director at Tytan Advertising in Tybee Island, Georgia. He's Chief Innovation Officer at Demand Magic in Los Angeles, and has a select group of clients he contracts with in his own design consultancy, Chris Jacobs Design. He's married to the phenomenal photographer and commercial actress, BeBe Jacobs.
